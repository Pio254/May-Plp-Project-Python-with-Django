Our Services

We provide advanced monitoring solutions to help farmers track soil moisture levels and make informed irrigation decisions. Our platform offers real-time sensor data collection and graph visualization for better field management.

Sensor Installation

We assist in the installation and setup of soil moisture sensors to ensure your field is constantly monitored.

Data Monitoring

Our platform helps you monitor data from multiple sensors spread across your farm, allowing you to view real-time information on soil conditions.

Visual Analytics

We provide visual analytics and graphs that help you interpret the collected data easily, leading to efficient irrigation decisions.
